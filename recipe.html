Chocolate chip cookie recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter or margarine ,softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4cup brown sugar ,packed
  • 2 eggs.large
  • 2 tea spoons vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups flour ,unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup walnuts or pecans,chopped (optionsl)
  • 2 cups KCC bittersweet 60% cacao baking chips

Baking instruction

  1. Heat in oven to 375'FsS
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt ;set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter with sugar ,and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in colour.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla,one at a time.
    Mix on low speed until incorporated.
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture.
    Stir in nuts and chocolate chips
  6. Drop by tablespoon onto ungrased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chip cookies are golden brown
  8. Enjoy!
